Our Activities

Activity 01

After School Club (ASC)

It helps to improve education for poor children by giving extra classes after school timings. It includes supplementing instructions in basic skills, such as learning Maths, Science, English etc.

Activity 02

Computer literacy and digital skills

Basic Computer, Tally and Spoken English: In today's world of rapid developments in technology, there are still hundreds and thousands of marginalized in India that neither have access nor afford computer literacy and skills to better their lives. The government schemes are mostly available online, which people from under-served communities are unable to access.so,We are dedicated to providing these young minds with the education and care they deserve a bright future.

Activity 03

Bridge Program

Spoorthi identifies school dropouts, out of school students and we bridge the gap and will enroll them in government schools or in low cost private schools.

Activity 04

Women Empowerment

Tailoring, Beautician and Soap making at home: This project intends to give skills training in tailoring, beautician course & soap making for sustainable livelihoods to women who are socially and economically disadvantaged. With this vocational skills training, women will live with dignity by earning money themselves.

Activity 05

Community survey
